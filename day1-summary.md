# Day 1 — Learning Summary

**Task:** Company & Product Ecosystem Exploration

## Objective

Understand the company's product ecosystem, explore the FLOCard platform, and learn how different types of users interact with it.

## 1. Platform Exploration

Explored the Home platform and the FLOCard platform to understand their purpose and features. FLOCard is a digital networking platform that lets users create a digital identity, share professional information, and support paperless networking.

## 2. Creating My FLOCard

- Signed up on the platform
- Completed my profile with personal and professional details
- Generated my digital FLOCard

This walked through the full registration and profile-creation flow end to end.

## 3. Card Exchange Activity

Exchanged my FLOCard with three team members, which showed how users can quickly connect and share contact information digitally instead of using paper business cards.

## 4. User Flows Explored

### Individual Flow
Individuals can create and manage their profiles, update their information, share their FLOCard, and build professional connections. Profile updates are automatically reflected on the shared card.

### Community Flow
Communities can create digital spaces where members connect, receive updates, participate in activities, and collaborate more effectively.

### Tree Planters Flow
Supports environmental initiatives — tree planters and organizations can showcase their work, connect with supporters, and promote sustainability projects.

## 5. Key Learnings

- The FLOCard ecosystem and how digital identity creation works
- How card exchange enables fast, paperless connection between users
- The distinct user journeys for **Individuals**, **Communities**, and **Tree Planters**
- How the platform actively encourages sustainable, paperless networking

## 6. Knowledge Sharing

Prepared a Substack post summarizing this Day 1 learning experience, as instructed during onboarding.

## Conclusion

This warm-up task gave practical, hands-on exposure to the FLOCard platform and built a strong foundation for understanding the company's products and services ahead of deeper technical onboarding.
