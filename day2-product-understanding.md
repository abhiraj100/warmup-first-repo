# Day 2 — Product Understanding Summary

**Topics covered:** Fennex, KidCare, and SharePoint Basic Concepts

## 1. Fennex — AI-Powered Safety Software

Fennex is an industrial software company (founded 2016, based in Aberdeen, Scotland) that builds AI-powered Health, Safety, Environment, Quality, and Operational Assurance (HSEQ) solutions for high-risk industries such as oil, gas, offshore, and renewable energy.

**Key points:**
- Flagship platform **FennexSafe** replaces paper-based safety processes with a digital system for incident reporting, audits, inspections, and compliance tracking
- **AI Predictive Safety** analyzes safety data to spot risks early and recommend preventive action
- Built on Microsoft technologies: Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Azure SQL, and Power BI
- **366Pi Technologies** is Fennex's engineering partner — building backend systems, APIs, dashboards, and AI features, and supporting testing, security, and releases

## 2. KidCare — Child Healthcare App

KidCare is a child healthcare app developed by 366Pi Technologies LLP. It helps parents manage their child's health and connect with doctors easily, rather than monitor device or internet usage.

**Key points:**
- **Appointment Booking** — book visits with doctors through the app
- **Expert Doctors** — access to qualified doctors and pediatricians for consultations
- **Vaccination Schedule** — track and manage the child's vaccination timeline
- **Symptom Checker** — get an early idea of possible health concerns
- **Health Resources** — articles and information about child health
- **Secure Communication** — safely message or consult with healthcare providers
- Rated 12+, no data shared with third parties, and data is encrypted in transit

## 3. SharePoint Basic Concepts

SharePoint is Microsoft's collaboration platform for storing, organizing, and sharing information across teams. Four core building blocks were covered:

### Sites
Collaborative workspaces for a team. Three types:
- **Team Site** — for group collaboration
- **Communication Site** — for broadcasting news to a wide audience
- **Hub Site** — for connecting related sites under one structure

### Document Libraries
Secure storage for files with version history, co-authoring, permissions, metadata, and check-in/check-out to prevent editing conflicts.

### Lists
Spreadsheet-like structured data storage (rows and columns) with custom columns, views, sorting/filtering, permissions, and alerts.

### Workflows
Automated sequences of actions (built mainly with Microsoft Power Automate) that handle approvals, notifications, task assignment, and data updates automatically.
